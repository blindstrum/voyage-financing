Everything You Get with Voyage

We built a mortgage company from scratch so we could fix everything that's broken. Here's what that looks like in practice.

📉

Lower Rates

On average, about 0.5% lower than the industry. On a $400,000 loan, that's roughly $120/month — or over $43,000 over the life of the loan. We keep our overhead lean and pass the savings directly to you.

💰

No Lender Fees

$0 in origination fees. $0 in application fees. $0 in processing fees. Most lenders charge $3,000–$5,000 in lender fees that get buried in your closing costs. We charge nothing. That money stays in your pocket.

🤝

Price Match Guarantee

Found a lower rate from a legitimate lender? Show us the Loan Estimate and we'll match it or beat it. We're confident in our pricing — and we want you to be too. No gimmicks, no fine print.

⏱️

Close On Time

98% of our loans close on or before the scheduled date. The industry average? About 40%. Late closings can cost you your dream home, your rate lock, or your earnest money. We don't let that happen.

🏠

Appraisal Coverage

If the appraisal comes in low, you won't face a surprise cash-to-close gap. We offer appraisal coverage on qualifying loans so a low valuation doesn't derail your purchase or force you to scramble for extra cash.

🛡️

On Your Side

Our loan advisors don't earn commission on upselling you a bigger loan or a higher rate. Their incentive is your satisfaction, period. When our interests are aligned with yours, you get better advice and a better outcome.
