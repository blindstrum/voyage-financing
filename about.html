Our Mission

To make homeownership radically more affordable, transparent, and accessible — by rebuilding the mortgage from the ground up.

What We Believe

The mortgage industry has spent decades perfecting the art of confusion — hiding fees in fine print, quoting rates that evaporate, and making the biggest purchase of your life feel like a trap. We believe it doesn't have to be this way. Voyage Financing exists to prove that a mortgage company can be radically transparent, genuinely affordable, and still build a thriving business. When we save you money, you trust us. When you trust us, you tell your friends. That's the only growth hack we need.

Born from Frustration

Our founders bought their first homes and were stunned by how broken the experience was. Hidden fees appeared at closing. Rates changed without explanation. Loan officers were impossible to reach. The closing date slipped — twice. And they were the lucky ones.

They looked at the numbers and realized the average borrower overpays by thousands of dollars because the system is designed for lender profit, not borrower benefit. Origination fees, inflated rates, and misaligned incentives are baked into the business model of every traditional lender.

So they built something different. Voyage Financing launched with three radical commitments: the lowest rates we can offer (not the highest we can get away with), zero lender fees, and technology that actually works for the borrower. No call centers. No bait-and-switch. No surprises at the closing table.

The result? Customers who save thousands, close on time, and actually enjoy the process. Realtors who trust us to deliver. And a growing team that's proving you can do well by doing right.

Our Journey

  • 2020 — Founded

    Voyage Financing incorporated with a simple thesis: mortgage borrowers deserve better. Two founders, one apartment, and a whiteboard full of ideas.

  • 2021 — Seed Round

    Raised $12M in seed funding to build the technology platform. Hired the first engineering and operations team. Applied for lending licenses in 15 states.

  • 2022 — Launch

    First loans closed. Launched with zero lender fees and rates that immediately undercut every major competitor. Early customers became our biggest advocates.

  • 2024 — Growth

    Expanded to 40+ states. Closed over $2B in mortgages. Achieved a 98% on-time close rate and a 5.0 average customer rating. Raised Series A to accelerate growth.

  • 2026 — Today

    Licensed in 48 states. Named Best Online Mortgage Lender. Continuing to push rates lower, close faster, and make homeownership accessible to more people every day.

Our Values

Transparency

We show you exactly what you'll pay, from the first quote to the closing table. No hidden fees, no surprise charges, no fine print that changes the deal. If we can't explain a cost in plain English, we don't charge it.

Technology

We built our platform from scratch — not bolted onto a legacy system from the 1990s. Our technology automates the tedious parts, catches errors before they delay your closing, and gives you 24/7 visibility into your loan status.

People

Technology handles the process. People handle the moments that matter. Our loan advisors are salaried professionals who earn nothing extra for steering you toward a more expensive product. Their success is measured by your satisfaction.
