Voyage Financing is a technology-first mortgage lender that offers lower rates, zero lender fees, and a faster closing process. We're licensed in 48 states and built our platform from scratch to make homebuying easier and more affordable.

Our online platform is available 24/7. Loan advisors are available Monday through Friday, 8 AM – 9 PM ET, and Saturday 9 AM – 5 PM ET. You can also reach us at (414) 309-6013.

We're currently licensed in 48 states plus the District of Columbia. The only states we don't yet serve are Hawaii and Alaska, though we're working on expanding there soon.

Three main differences: (1) Our rates are typically 0.5% lower than the industry average because of our low-overhead model. (2) We charge zero lender fees — no origination, application, or processing fees. (3) We close 98% of loans on time, compared to the industry average of about 40%. Start by clicking "Get Started" and completing our online application. It takes about 10 minutes. You'll provide basic income, employment, and asset information. We'll run a soft credit check (no impact on your score) and give you a pre-approval letter, often within minutes.

Our initial pre-approval uses a soft credit inquiry, which does not affect your credit score. A hard inquiry is only pulled when you formally apply for a specific loan, and multiple mortgage inquiries within a 14–45 day window are treated as a single inquiry by the credit bureaus.

For conventional loans, we generally require a minimum credit score of 620. FHA loans may be available with scores as low as 580. The best rates are typically available to borrowers with scores of 740 and above. We encourage you to check — there's no cost and no impact on your score.

Our pre-approval letters are valid for 90 days. If your letter expires before you find a home, we can quickly re-verify your information and issue a new one. Your rate may change based on current market conditions at the time of renewal.

Because Voyage charges zero lender fees, our rate and APR are typically very close together — unlike many competitors where the APR can be significantly higher than the advertised rate.

Most Voyage loans close in 21–30 days from contract to keys. Some close in as few as 14 days. The industry average is 45–50 days, with frequent delays. Our 98% on-time close rate means the date on your contract is the date you'll move in.

A rate lock guarantees your interest rate for a set period (typically 30–60 days) while your loan is being processed. If rates rise during that time, your locked rate stays the same. It protects you from market volatility between application and closing.

Most borrowers lock their rate once they have an accepted offer on a home. You can lock at application or wait — but waiting means you're exposed to rate movements. Your loan advisor can help you decide based on current market conditions and your closing timeline.

Voyage offers a float-down option on qualifying locks. If rates drop significantly after you've locked, you may be able to take advantage of the lower rate. Specific terms and thresholds apply — your loan advisor will explain the details when you lock.
