Glossary of terms

Rate
The interest rate on your mortgage, which determines your monthly payment. Rates change daily based on market conditions.
APR
Annual Percentage Rate includes your interest rate plus any additional costs (like points), giving you the true cost of borrowing.
Closing costs
Additional services you'll need when buying a home, such as appraisal and title. We've estimated these so you can understand the full financial impact.
Points / credits
Points and credits determine your closing costs and interest rate. Paying points means you pay more at closing to get a lower rate. Taking credits means you pay less at closing but get a higher rate.
Lender fees
Fees charged by the lender for processing your loan. Voyage charges $0 in lender fees — always.
