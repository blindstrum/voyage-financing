Rate Glossary

Interest Rate

The percentage charged by the lender for borrowing money. This is the base cost of your loan and determines how much interest accrues each month on your remaining balance.

APR (Annual Percentage Rate)

The total yearly cost of borrowing, including the interest rate plus other charges like prepaid interest and third-party fees. APR gives you a more complete picture of what you'll pay.

Closing Costs

Fees paid when your mortgage closes, including appraisal, title insurance, escrow, and recording fees. Typically 2–5% of the home price. Voyage charges $0 in lender fees.

Points & Credits

Discount points are upfront fees you pay to lower your interest rate (1 point = 1% of the loan). Lender credits work in reverse — the lender covers some closing costs in exchange for a slightly higher rate.
