Loan Products

Find the right loan for your mission.

Conventional Loans

Standard financing with as little as 3% down. Best for borrowers with good credit and steady income looking for flexible terms.

  • 3% Down
  • PMI Cancellable
  • 620+ Credit

FHA Loans

Government-backed loans ideal for first-time buyers or lower credit scores. 3.5% down with 580+ credit.

  • 3.5% Down
  • 580+ Credit
  • First-Time Buyers

VA Loans

For veterans, active-duty service members, and eligible spouses. Zero down payment and no PMI required.

  • 0% Down
  • No PMI
  • Veterans Only

USDA Loans

For rural and suburban homebuyers. Zero down payment, low rates, and flexible eligibility — income limits apply.

  • 0% Down
  • Rural Areas
  • Income Limits

Jumbo Loans

For properties exceeding conforming loan limits. Competitive rates for luxury homes and high-value purchases.

  • High Loan Amounts
  • Competitive Rates

Fixed-Rate Mortgages

Stable monthly payments with a consistent interest rate for the entire life of your loan. Available in 15, 20, and 30-year terms.

  • 15/20/30 Year
  • Stable Payments

Adjustable Rate Mortgages (ARM)

Starts with a lower initial rate that adjusts over time. Great for short-term ownership or when rates are expected to decline.

  • Lower Initial Rate
  • 5/7/10 Year ARM

Licensed in 3 States

State-specific programs to save you more.

Wisconsin (WHEDA)

Wisconsin Housing and Economic Development Authority programs for first-time and repeat buyers.

  • Advantage Conventional & FHA — competitive rates
  • Easy Close DPA — up to 6% of purchase price
  • Capital Access — up to $7,500 in down payment help
  • Tax Advantage — Mortgage Credit Certificate program

Iowa (IFA)

Iowa Finance Authority programs helping Iowans achieve homeownership.

  • FirstHome Program — below-market rates for first-time buyers
  • Homes for Iowans — available to all buyers
  • DPA Grant — $2,500 grant (no repayment)
  • Military Homeownership — additional benefits for veterans

Florida (FHFC)

Florida Housing Finance Corporation programs with generous assistance.

  • FL Assist — $10,000 deferred second mortgage at 0%
  • Hometown Heroes — up to $35,000 for community workers
  • HFA Preferred/Advantage — competitive rates + DPA
  • Salute Our Soldiers — specialized veteran program
