$750K Mortgage Limit

The IRS allows mortgage interest deductions only on the first $750,000 of debt for loans originated after December 15, 2017.

Property Tax Deduction

State and local property taxes (SALT) are deductible up to $10,000 per year ($5,000 if married filing separately).

Itemize vs. Standard

You benefit from itemizing only when your total itemized deductions exceed the standard deduction for your filing status.
