How It Works

Your home loan journey in 6 steps.

From first call to closing day — here's exactly what to expect.

01

Pre-Approval

Share your financials and we'll tell you exactly how much you can borrow and at what rate. Takes about 15 minutes.

02

Find Your Home

Shop with confidence knowing your budget. Your pre-approval letter makes sellers take you seriously.

03

Lock Your Rate

Once you're under contract, we lock in your interest rate to protect you from market fluctuations.

04

Loan Processing

We verify your details, order the appraisal, and coordinate with title. You'll have a dedicated processor.

05

Underwriting

Our underwriters review everything for final approval. We keep you updated every step of the way.

06

Closing Day

Sign the final documents, get your keys, and officially become a homeowner. Welcome home.
